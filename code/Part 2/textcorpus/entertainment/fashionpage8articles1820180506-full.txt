Sarah Christine Sullivan and Lt. James Kilburn Compton were married May 5 in Washington. The Rev. Agustn Lpez performed the ceremony at the Roman Catholic Cathedral of St. Matthew the Apostle.The bride, 29, will continue to use her name professionally. She is a director and a financial policy and investment analyst at Capstone, a company in Washington that advises hedge funds and private investment firms on regulatory and other matters. She graduated from the University of Virginia and received a masters degree in political economy from the London School of Economics and Political Science.She is a daughter of Susan McLain Sullivan and Bartholomew D. Sullivan of Falls Church, Va. The brides father is the communications director in Washington for Representative Stephen I. Cohen, Democrat of Tennessee. Her mother is an English and creative writing teacher at Oakton High School in Vienna, Va.Lieutenant Compton, 27, is known as Jake. He serves in the Navy as a staff officer for personnel planning and policy for the nuclear propulsion program, at the Naval Support Facility in Arlington, Va. He graduated with merit from the United States Naval Academy.He is a son of Elizabeth K. Compton and Eric P. Grubman of Washington Township, N.J. The grooms father is the executive vice president for strategic projects at the National Football League in New York.The couple met in 2012, in Charleston, S.C., at the dinner party of a mutual friend.
