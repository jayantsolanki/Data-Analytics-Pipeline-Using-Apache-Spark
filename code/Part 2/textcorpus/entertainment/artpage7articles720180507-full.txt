MEMPHIS, Tenn.  Memphis police are appealing to the public to help investigators find out who fatally shot two people and wounded four others near a high-rise building in midtown.The department tweeted Monday that two of the four victims have been released from the hospital and two others remain in critical condition.Police said late Sunday that there had been a shooting with multiple victims near the art deco Crosstown Concourse.WREG reports witnesses said they heard about 13 gunshots. They also said they heard a loud boom which may have come from a car accident that followed the shooting.Police say the suspect fled in a gold four-door sedan. They do not yet have a motive.
