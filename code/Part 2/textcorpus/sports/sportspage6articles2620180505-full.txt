ESTORIL, Portugal  Frances Tiafoe of the United States upset defending champion Pablo Carreno 6-2, 6-3 to reach the Estoril Open final on Saturday.The 20-year-old Tiafoe will face local favorite Joao Sousa in Sunday's final after the Portuguese beat Stefanos Tsitsipas 6-4, 1-6, 7-6 (4).Tiafoe converted four of seven break chances to beat the second-seeded Carreno on the outdoor clay court.Tiafoe won his first ATP Tour title at Delray Beach in February.The 19-year-old Tsitsipas committed five double faults, to go with four aces, in the loss to Sousa.Sousa will be seeking his third career title and his first since 2015.
