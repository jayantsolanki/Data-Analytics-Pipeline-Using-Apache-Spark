PARIS, May 7  Olympique Lyonnais goalkeeper Anthony Lopess suspension has been increased from three to five games following a brawl at the end of a Ligue 1 match against Olympique de Marseille, the French Football Federation said on Monday. The incidents happened at the Stade Velodrome on March 18.Marseille defender Adil Ramis three-match suspension was confirmed.  (Reporting by Julien Pretot, editing by Ed Osmond)
