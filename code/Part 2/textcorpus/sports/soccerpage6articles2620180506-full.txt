(Reuters) - Cardiff City were promoted to the Premier League after a goalless draw against Reading on Sunday secured them second place in England's second tier Championship. Cardiff finished with 90 points, two points ahead of third-placed Fulham, who suffered a shock 3-1 defeat at Birmingham City on the final day of the campaign. Cardiff re-enter the top-flight for the first time since the 2013-14 season. (Reporting by Aditi Prakash in Bengaluru; Editing by Toby Davis)
