CHICAGO  James Shields of the Chicago White Sox has lost his no-hit bid with one out in the seventh inning against the Minnesota Twins.Eduardo Escobar singled to right center field for the Twins' first hit Sunday.The 36-year-old Shields had struck out five and walked two before giving up a hit.
