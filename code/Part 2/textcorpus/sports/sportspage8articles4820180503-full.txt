THE COLONY, Texas  The first round of the LPGA North Texas Classic has been called off because of heavy morning rain. Only 34 of the 144 golfers completed at least one hole.Play was stopped Thursday 69 minutes after the first groups teed off. The decision to resume play Friday was made during mid-afternoon, hours after the rain stopped and sunshine bathed the course. Thursday's scores were thrown out.A tournament official says no decision on the schedule for the weekend's final three rounds will be made until the first round is completed. Friday's forecast calls for more rain.The event is in its first season at Old American Golf Club.
