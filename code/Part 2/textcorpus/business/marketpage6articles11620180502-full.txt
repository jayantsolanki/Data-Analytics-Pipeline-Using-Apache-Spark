(Reuters) - Tens of thousands of people will descend on Omaha, Nebraska this weekend for the annual shareholder weekend for Berkshire Hathaway Inc, the conglomerate that billionaire Warren Buffett has run since 1965.Buffett calls the three-day weekend, the largest corporate gathering in the United States, "Woodstock for Capitalists."
