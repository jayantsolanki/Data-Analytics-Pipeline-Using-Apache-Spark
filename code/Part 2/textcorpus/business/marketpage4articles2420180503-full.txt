LONDON  Banco Bilbao Vizcaya Argentaria (BBVA) said on Thursday it has issued a 1 billion euro (881 million pounds) green bond, the largest ever by a Eurozone financial institution.The seven-year non-preferred senior green bond makes BBVA the first Spanish bank to issue such a debt instrument, it said in a statement.Green bonds are a growing category of fixed-income securities which raise capital for projects with environmental benefits.This year, BBVA hopes to issue between 2.5 billion and 3.5 billion euros of green bonds, depending on market conditions.
